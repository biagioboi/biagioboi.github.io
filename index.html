Bio

Currently, I'm a PhD Student in Computer Science at the University of Salerno (UNISA). I received Master Degree in Computer Science, curricula Internet of Things (IoT) under the supervision of my advisor Prof. Christian Esposito discussing a thesis on Optimization of energy harvesting and data transmission in 5G cellular networks. This interesting topic is only one side of my research interests, which include networking, security and IoT.
